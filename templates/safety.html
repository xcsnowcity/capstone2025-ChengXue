üíª Digital Safety

Using This Website Safely

  • Quick Exit: Use the red "Quick Exit" button or press Escape key
  • Private Browsing: Use incognito/private mode when possible
  • Clear History: Delete browser history after use
  • Safe Computer: Only use on computers that your abuser cannot access

Phone & Computer Safety

  • Change passwords on accounts your abuser might access
  • Check for tracking apps on your devices
  • Be aware of shared cloud storage (photos, documents)
  • Consider using a trusted friend's device if yours isn't safe

üè† Personal Safety Planning

If You're Still Living Together

  • Identify safe rooms with exits and avoid kitchens/bathrooms
  • Keep important documents and cash in a safe place
  • Have a code word with trusted friends/family for emergencies
  • Memorize important phone numbers

Emergency Bag

Keep these items accessible if you need to leave quickly:

  • Identification (passport, driver's license)
  • Important documents (birth certificates, marriage certificate)
  • Money and bank cards
  • Medications
  • Children's items (if applicable)
  • Change of clothes
  • Keys (house, car)
