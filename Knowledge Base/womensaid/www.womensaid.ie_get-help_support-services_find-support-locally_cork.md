# Local Support Services in Cork
## Cuanlee Refuge
### Location
Cork
### Organisational Overview
Provides safe and secure crisis accommodation for women and children experiencing domestic abuse. Help women who wish to move on and live independently, free from violence. 
### Services Provided
Crisis Accomodation, 24 Hour Help-line , Child-Adolescent Services, Outreach Social Work, Domestic Abuse Workshops, Court Accompaniment & Preparation Service, Safety Planning 
### Helpline Number
021-4277698
### Helpline Opening Hours
24 hours, 7 days per week
### Address
Cuanlee Refuge Kyrls Quay Cork City T12 HK74
### Email
info@cuanleerefuge.org
### Website
<https://cuanleerefuge.org/>
## OSS Cork
### Location
Cork
### Organisational Overview
Primary objective is to achieve the safety and security of our clients and any of their dependents via the provision of information, support, advice, advocacy and accompaniment 
### Services Provided
Helpline, Safety Planning, Financial, Housing, Legal, Health and Other Information, Advocacy and Accompaniment, One to One support 
### Helpline Number

### Helpline Opening Hours

## Mná Feasa
### Location
Cork
### Organisational Overview
Was set up primarily by women who have themselves survived domestic violence. A community based project run by trained and experienced staff to help victims of domestic violence.
### Services Provided
Helpline, Court Accompaniment, Support Groups, Support & Information, Outreach, Schools Education Programme, Alternative Health Care Programme, Text Service for Women

## West Cork Women Against Violence Project
### Location
West Cork
### Organisational Overview
Provides a safe, confidential listening, information and support service to women and their children in West Cork experiencing domestic violence, while also challenging social, political and judicial tolerance of violence against women. 
### Services Provided
Helpline, Advocacy, Court Accompaniment, Safe house, Subsidised Counselling, Education, Training, Programme for schools, Support Groups, Parenting Support Group, Children's Therapy Supports 
### Helpline Number
1800 203 136
### Helpline Opening Hours
Monday to Friday: 9am to 5pm
### Address

### Office Number

### Office Opening Hours
