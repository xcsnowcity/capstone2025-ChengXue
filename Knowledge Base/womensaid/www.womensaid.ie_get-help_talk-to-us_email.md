You can email the Helpline team at helpline@womensaid.ie for basic information. However, we can’t provide ongoing support via email.
Our goal is to reply to all emails within 3 working days.
When you email us, it is confidential. This means you do not have to give any personal information such as:
  * name
  * address
  * telephone number


Please note that Women’s Aid operates under the Children First National Guidance for the Protection and Welfare of Children 2017. [Read more.](https://www.womensaid.ie/get-help/talk-to-us/service-user-policies/)
**Please note that we can not open attachments to emails.**
We will be unable to respond to emails addressed to helpline@womensaid.ie from the 20th December to the 2nd of January 2025. During this time the 24 Hr. National Freephone Helpline is available on 1800 341 900.
