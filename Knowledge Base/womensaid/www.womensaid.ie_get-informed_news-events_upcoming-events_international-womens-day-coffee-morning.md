# International Women’s Day Coffee Morning
Come together this International Women’s Day 2025 and host a coffee morning with friends, family, colleagues or neighbours in solidarity, in celebration, and in support of women everywhere.
## Saturday 8th March 2025
![](https://www.womensaid.ie/app/uploads/2025/02/Thank-you-to-our-amazing-community-of-supporters-Website.png)
#### Celebrate the women in your life this International Women’s Day **by hosting a coffee morning in support of Women’s Aid**
Every five minutes, someone reaches out to Women’s Aid for information and support. Host a coffee morning to support us and make a real difference this International Women’s Day. We’re delighted to have the coffee morning sponsored by our kind partner, Imbibe Coffee Roasters.
#### **How to host your International Women’s Day coffee morning**
To take part, all you have to do is:
  * [Sign up](https://fundraising.womensaid.ie/event/coffee-morning/home) to host a coffee morning and get your online fundraising page.
  * Get your **FREE** coffee morning kit in the post from us.
  * Invite as many people as possible to join in and raise awareness and funds for women subjected to domestic violence.
