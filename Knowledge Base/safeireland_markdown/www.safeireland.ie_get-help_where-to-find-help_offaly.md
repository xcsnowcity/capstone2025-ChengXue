## Offaly Domestic Violence Support Services
County: Offaly Tel: 057 9351886 Helpline: 057 935 1886 Email: info@odvss.ie Website: [www.odvss.ie](http://www.odvss.ie/) Access Hours: Monday to Friday, 9.30am to 5pm
### Ethos/Main Aims of Organisation:
To provide support and services for victims of domestic violence and their children at a local level and campaign for societal and legislative changes that will lead to the elimination of violence and abuse’.
### Services Provided:
  * Information
  * Emotional Supports
  * Safety Supports
  * Practical Supports
  * Court and Other Accompaniment
  * Advocacy
  * Referral
  * Outreach
  * Transport to Refuge (if required)
